About SDG 7 – Affordable & Clean Energy

SDG 7 is one of the 17 Global Goals set by the United Nations. It focuses on ensuring access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all.

Energy is the backbone of development: it powers schools, hospitals, transportation, and homes. Without clean energy, countries face pollution, inequality, and environmental destruction.

The Main Targets of SDG 7

  • Ensure universal access to affordable and reliable modern energy.
  • Increase the share of renewable energy worldwide.
  • Improve energy efficiency globally.
  • Promote access to clean energy research and technology.
  • Enhance international cooperation.

Why SDG 7 is Urgent

Today, many communities still face energy poverty. People cook using wood or coal, children study without lights, and hospitals rely on unstable power sources. Achieving SDG 7 can transform lives on a massive scale.

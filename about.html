About This Website

This website was created to support learning and awareness about Sustainable Development Goal 7 (SDG 7): Affordable and Clean Energy. It was designed for students, educators, and anyone interested in understanding how energy impacts our daily lives and the environment.

The goal of this project is to present clean, easy-to-read information about why energy matters, what problems the world currently faces, and what solutions exist to build a more sustainable future.

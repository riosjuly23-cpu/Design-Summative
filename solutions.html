Types of Clean Energy

1. Solar Energy

Solar energy is one of the most popular clean energy sources. Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity. Many homes and schools around the world use solar panels because they are environmentally friendly and reduce electricity bills.

2. Wind Energy

Wind turbines use the power of moving air to produce electricity. Wind farms can generate large amounts of renewable energy without causing pollution.

3. Hydropower

Hydropower uses the movement of water to create energy. Large dams generate electricity for entire cities. It is reliable and renewable.

What Schools Can Do

Schools can take small but effective steps to save energy. One simple method is switching off lights and fans when not needed. Replacing old bulbs with LED lights also saves energy and money.

Schools can also start eco clubs, conduct clean energy workshops, or even install small solar panels to power parts of the building. These actions help students learn about sustainability and make a real difference.

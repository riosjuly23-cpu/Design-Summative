Types of Clean Energy

Renewable energy comes from natural sources that are constantly replenished. These technologies do not run out and produce little to no pollution, making them essential for a sustainable global future.

Solar Power

Solar energy converts sunlight into electricity using solar panels. It is one of the fastest-growing renewable energy solutions and is affordable, scalable, and ideal for both homes and industries.

Wind Energy

Wind turbines harness wind to generate electricity. Wind farms can be built on land or offshore, producing massive amounts of clean energy without pollution.

Hydropower

Hydropower uses flowing water to create electricity. It is one of the most reliable energy sources because rivers provide a constant power flow.
